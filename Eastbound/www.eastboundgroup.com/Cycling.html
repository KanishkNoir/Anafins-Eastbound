Cycling

❮ ❯

Do you ever feel you are going too fast on a trip to actually not see anything while you travel in India? That’s because you probably are. Indian cities are all about the kind of feelings they give you as you tour the place and there is nothing that will match the experience of a place through a cycle tour in India. The very essence of travel is all about seeing and taking in what people of a certain region do every day and the beautiful historical buildings you are to see, combined with the cultures and everyday life of India.


Highlights:

Ride down a trail with forested meadow which is overlooks the valley of Rampur.

Ride the ‘World’s most treacherous road’. Baspa valley in Kinnaur has a high altitude road overlooking one of the deepest gorges in the valley.

Ride to Kalpa, one of the smallest hamlets in the Kinnaur region. With the view of the snowy Kinner Kailash range and the serpent roads of Reckong Peo.

Ride to the village of Rohgi, famously located at the highest point of a gorge

Ride to Tabo Monastery, a 1000 year old Clay monastery protected by UNESCO as a world heritage site.
