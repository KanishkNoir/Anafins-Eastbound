Trekking

❮ ❯

There are many hidden places in India that cannot be reached by car, train, bus or any other means of transport. In such cases, your own two legs – the natural means of transport – can take you forward. Every bit of land is accessible by this means in the most fuel-efficient manner.

Most of the treks delve deep into nature with the rivers, flowers, birds, animals, lush green trees, forts, caves, waterfalls, etc. There’s ceaseless amazement at what mother nature might present to you. Last but not the least, pure air, which is a luxury nowadays for those of us who reside in concrete city jungles.

Highlights:

Trekking in search of the ghost cat.

Climb to Nako which is famous for it’s sacred waterbody ‘Nako Lake’ and 1000 yrs old Monastery.

Explore the local Monastery of the village.

Trek to the beautiful village of Langza , also known as The Fossil Village.

Trek down to the lone lake ‘Chandra Tal’ aka ‘The Lake of the Moon’ to enjoy a lovely picnic
